# Responsible Use

A few Echo features are powerful enough to be worth a quick word on using them well.

- **Profile/account lookup commands** return information that's already publicly visible on the platform being checked — they don't bypass anyone's privacy settings. Please don't use them to build a profile on someone without their knowledge, or for harassment.
- **View-once recovery** defeats the sender's expectation that a photo/video is single-view. Use it on your own messages, or with the sender's knowledge — not to secretly archive other people's disappearing content.
- **Group moderation tools** (kick, anti-link, warnings, etc.) are for admins managing their own communities, not for targeting people outside them.

If you spot a bug, unexpected behavior, or a security concern, reach out privately via **[t.me/silentium_01](https://t.me/silentium_01)** rather than posting details publicly — gives us a chance to fix it before anyone else finds it.
