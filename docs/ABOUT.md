# What is Echo Oblivion?

Echo Oblivion is a WhatsApp bot — but built to feel less like a script replying to keywords, and more like a system running quietly behind your chats, ready the moment you need it.

## The idea

Most WhatsApp bots do one thing: download videos, or moderate groups, or answer questions. Echo does all three at once, and ties them together so they feel like one product instead of three bolted-on scripts:

- Talk to it and it **remembers the conversation** and responds like it understands context, not just keywords.
- Ask for a song and it arrives **properly tagged** — title, artist, cover art — like it came from a real music service.
- Drop it into a group and it becomes a **standing defense system** — link filtering, mention spam protection, delete tracking, admin-abuse prevention — all running silently until it's needed.
- Every reply, menu, and broadcast is wrapped in a **consistent visual identity** — styled cards, not plain grey text bubbles.

## Who it's for

- **Individuals** who want an AI assistant living inside WhatsApp instead of a separate app.
- **Group admins** who are tired of manually moderating links, spam, and rogue admin actions.
- **Communities** that want a consistent, branded presence — broadcasts, welcome messages, and menus that all look like they came from the same place.

## How it's run

Echo runs as a hosted system — you don't install anything, and you don't need a server, a laptop staying on, or any technical setup. Pairing your number is the entire process. See **[docs/PAIRING.md](PAIRING.md)**.
